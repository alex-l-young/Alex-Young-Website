Ithaca, NY Climate

Visualizing local weather patterns from Cornell University data.

The city of Ithaca is located in Central New York at the southern tip of Cayuga Lake. Somewhat moderated by the large body of water, the local climate is generally cool year round. The plots below show historical trends for temperature, precipitation, and snowfall.
