Bouncy Balls

I've been fascinated with the power of p5.js as of late. It's a neat little package that allows you to draw and move shapes in an animated way. As far as limitations, it seems like the ceiling is my own creativity and ability to code up the numerical physics implementations. My end goal is to eventually put together a little interactive flood simulation, but for now, enjoy the bouncy balls.
